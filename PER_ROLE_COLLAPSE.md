# 📦 Per-Role Collapse Feature

## ✅ Feature Implemented

Added individual collapse/expand chevron buttons to **each role card** in the Rôles & Permissions section.

---

## 🎯 What Changed

### Before
- Single global toggle button for all roles
- All roles expanded or collapsed together
- Less flexibility

### After ✨
- **Per-role collapse buttons** - Each role has its own chevron
- **Independent control** - Collapse some roles, expand others
- **Persistent state** - Each role's state is saved individually
- **Better UX** - Focus on specific roles without clutter

## 💡 How It Works

### User Actions
1. **Collapse a role:** Click the chevron (▲) → Card collapses
2. **Expand a role:** Click the chevron (▼) → Card expands
3. **Each role is independent** - Collapse Role A, keep Role B expanded

### Visual Indicators
- **Expanded:** Chevron points up (▲) - "Réduire" (Reduce)
- **Collapsed:** Chevron points down (▼) - "Développer" (Expand)
- **Collapsed card:** Gray background for visual distinction

### HTML Structure (Generated by JS)
```html

  <div style="display:flex;justify-content:space-between;">
    <div>
      <h3>Role Name</h3>
      <p>5/13 permissions actives</p>
    </div>
    <button class="role-collapse-btn" onclick="toggleRoleCard(1, 'Role Name')">
      <i class="fas fa-chevron-down"></i>
    </button>
  </div>
  <div class="role-content" style="display:none">
    <!-- Permissions grid (hidden when collapsed) -->
  </div>
</div>
```

## 🚀 Usage Example

### Scenario: Managing 5 Roles
1. **Initial view:** All 5 roles expanded (lots of scrolling)
2. **Collapse 3 roles:** Click ▲ on "Caisse", "Serveur", "Stagiaire"
3. **Result:** Only "Super Admin" and "Manager" remain expanded
4. **Page refresh:** Your collapsed roles stay collapsed!

### Keyboard Shortcuts (Future Enhancement)
- `Tab` to navigate to chevron button
- `Enter` or `Space` to toggle
- `Esc` to close expanded role
